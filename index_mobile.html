About Us:

delivering to small towns and rural areas.


Have you been in an area where your only option is to get to their restaurant to get their food? (Rural areas) Well, we have a solution to that!

“Grubb To Go” is a delivery service similar to Uber Eats, but for rural areas that do not offer delivery options for their restaurants.



"Grubb To Go" is a newly-founded food delivery service that is for rural areas where there is not very much (or any) delivery options, and was founded in the year [REDACTED] by Logan Huffman and Kaden Huffman who are both from a small town called Wingham which is a rural area that sparked the idea to start Grubb To Go.
